# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

_最后更新 / Last updated: 2026-06-09_

---

## 中文

**飞书文档AI助手**（以下简称"本扩展"）是一个开源、**无自有后端**的 Chrome 扩展。作者**不收集、不存储、不传输**你的任何数据。

### 1. 我们收集什么
**不收集任何数据。** 本扩展没有作者运营的服务器，不做统计/埋点/广告/追踪，不会把你的任何信息发送给作者或第三方分析服务。

### 2. 数据存在哪
以下内容**仅保存在你本机**的浏览器存储（`chrome.storage.local`），**加密存储**，从不上传：
- 你的飞书 **App ID / App Secret**（App Secret 经设备密钥加密）；
- 飞书 **user_access_token / refresh_token**（OAuth 登录凭据，加密）；
- 你的**大模型 API Key**（加密）；
- 你的设置、已保存的看板/演示等本地数据。

卸载扩展或在设置中清除，即彻底删除上述数据。

### 3. 数据发往何处（仅两类，均由你发起）
本扩展运行时只在你**主动发起操作**时产生两类出站请求：
1. **飞书开放平台 API**（`*.feishu.cn`）：以**你本人的身份**（user_access_token）读写你授权的多维表格/文档/电子表格——即你让助手做的操作。本扩展**只用你本人身份**，绝不使用应用/租户身份越权。
2. **你自己配置的大模型服务**（如 DeepSeek、OpenAI 兼容接口）：为完成你发起的任务，发送**必要的、有字符上限的**内容给**你自己填写的**模型服务。模型地址、密钥都由你配置，本扩展不代选、不中转。

本扩展**不会**把数据发给作者或任何第三方分析/广告服务。

### 4. 权限说明
- `identity`：仅用于走飞书 OAuth、获取**你本人的** user_access_token。
- `storage`：本机加密保存上述凭据与设置。
- `host_permissions: *.feishu.cn`、`activeTab`、`scripting`、`content_scripts`：在飞书页面注入侧边栏与读取当前页面上下文，以执行你的指令。
- `contextMenus` / `commands`：右键/快捷键触发"剪藏到飞书"等功能。
- `sidePanel`：提供侧边栏界面。

### 5. AI 生成代码的隔离
助手生成的可视化/页面代码在**隔离沙箱**（opaque origin）中运行，且沙箱 **无任何网络出站权限**（`connect-src 'none'`）——生成的代码即便想外发数据也发不出去。

### 6. 儿童
本扩展面向职场办公用户，不面向 13 岁以下儿童。

### 7. 变更与联系
政策更新会修改本文件顶部日期。问题请在仓库提交 issue：
https://github.com/scott987-cmd/feishu-doc-ai-assistant/issues

## English

**Feishu Doc AI Assistant** ("the Extension") is an open-source, **backend-less** Chrome extension. The author **does not collect, store, or transmit** any of your data.

### 1. What we collect
**Nothing.** There is no author-operated server, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. None of your information is sent to the author or any third-party analytics service.

### 2. Where data lives
The following is stored **only on your device** (`chrome.storage.local`), **encrypted**, and never uploaded:
- Your Feishu **App ID / App Secret** (the secret is encrypted with a device key);
- Feishu **user_access_token / refresh_token** (OAuth credentials, encrypted);
- Your **LLM API key** (encrypted);
- Your settings and locally saved dashboards/decks.

Uninstalling the extension or clearing settings permanently deletes the above.

### 3. Where data is sent (only two destinations, all user-initiated)
At runtime the Extension makes outbound requests only when **you initiate an action**, to two destinations:
1. **Feishu Open Platform API** (`*.feishu.cn`): acting **as you** (user_access_token) to read/write the Base/Doc/Sheet you authorized — i.e. the operations you ask the assistant to perform. The Extension uses **your identity only**, never an app/tenant identity.
2. **Your own configured LLM service** (e.g. DeepSeek, any OpenAI-compatible endpoint): to fulfill your task, the **necessary, character-capped** content is sent to the model service **you configured**. The endpoint and key are yours; the Extension does not choose or relay them.

The Extension does **not** send data to the author or any third-party analytics/ad service.

### 4. Permissions
- `identity`: solely to run Feishu OAuth and obtain **your own** user_access_token.
- `storage`: to store the above credentials/settings encrypted on-device.
- `host_permissions: *.feishu.cn`, `activeTab`, `scripting`, `content_scripts`: to inject the side panel into Feishu pages and read the current page context to execute your commands.
- `contextMenus` / `commands`: right-click / shortcut triggers (e.g. "clip to Feishu").
- `sidePanel`: provides the side-panel UI.

### 5. Isolation of AI-generated code
Visualization/page code generated by the assistant runs in an **isolated sandbox** (opaque origin) that has **no network egress** (`connect-src 'none'`) — generated code cannot exfiltrate data even if it tried.

### 6. Children
The Extension targets workplace/productivity users and is not directed to children under 13.

### 7. Changes & contact
Updates change the date at the top of this file. Questions: open an issue at
https://github.com/scott987-cmd/feishu-doc-ai-assistant/issues
